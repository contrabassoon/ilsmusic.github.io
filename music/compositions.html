Compositions

Bassoon Sonata no. 1 (2020)

I wrote this piece near the end of my senior year of high school to perform on a senior recital. This performance features me playing bassoon with synthetic piano accompaniment.

Duet in Eb (2019)

This is a short and simple duet for two bassoons I wrote. Versions also exist for flute and bassoon and for bassoon and bass clarinet. In this recording, both parts are played by me on bassoon.

WIP Sonata

Only the first movement of this sonata has been completed. It features more harmonic variation than my other pieces, and the finished work will also include movements of oboe. This performance features me playing bassoon with synthetic piano accompaniment.

Greensleeves/Carol of the Bells

This piece is a medley of the traditional songs "Greensleeves" and "Carol of the Bells" for bassoon quintet. In this performance, all five parts are played by me.

Arrangements

Funeral March of a Marionette - Charles Gounod

One of my first arrangements, this arrangement is for bassoon quartet and has been performed several times since I wrote it. In this performance, all four parts are played by me.

Symphony no. 8 - Antonin Dvorak

This is one of my largest arrangements to date and was written over the course of several weeks. This arrangement is for double reed octet, and in this recording, all oboe and bassoon parts are played by me.
